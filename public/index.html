HOW FOOTBALL SQUARES WORK

The Board

The board playing area is a 10 x 10 grid. At the top of the board is the name of the home team and along the side is the name of the visiting team.

Between the playing area and the team names you’ll find 10 empty boxes; this is called the score area. The score area is left empty until the playing area is completely filled.

The Game

Players will purchase empty boxes for the price set by the admin of the board.

Once all the boxes have been purchased, each score area will be assigned a random number from 0-9 for both teams.

The Winners

Now the board is complete, all that is left is to find out who the winners are! Winners are determined based on the score at the end of each quarter.

The winner is determined by looking at the last number in each of the teams' score, and then matching those numbers on the grid and seeing which square intersects those two numbers.

If the score at the end of the game is Home 33, and Visitors 24, then the square on the grid that has Home with number 3, and Visitor number 4, would be the winner.
